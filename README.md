# eniac-magist-partnership-analysis

> **Should Eniac partner with Magist to enter the Brazilian market?**  
> SQL and Tableau analysis evaluating Eniac's potential partnership with Magist to enter the Brazilian market.

---

## Project Overview

Eniac is a Spanish online marketplace specialising in Apple-compatible accessories, with an average item price of €540 and an average order value of €710. As part of its expansion into Brazil, Eniac evaluated a potential partnership with **Magist** — a Brazilian SaaS company that connects small and medium sellers to major marketplaces.

This project analyses Magist's transactional database to determine whether the partnership is commercially viable for Eniac.

**Verdict: Not to Partner.**

## Database

The Magist database covers **September 2016 to August 2018** (25 months) and includes:

**Tech categories analysed:**
`computers`, `computers_accessories`, `electronics`, `telephony`, `fixed_telephony`, `consoles_games`, `audio`, `tablets_printing_image`, `pc_gamer`

## Key Findings

### 1. Eniac Vs Magist

Comparison window: **Apr 2017 – Mar 2018**

| Metric | Eniac | Magist Tech |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Total Revenue | €14M | €1.24M |
| Avg Monthly Revenue | €1.17M | €103K |
| Avg Order Price | €710 | €123.95 |
| Avg Item Price | €540 | €109.70 |

Magist's entire tech revenue per month is only **9% of Eniac's monthly target**.  
The avg item price gap is **~5x** — reflecting a fundamentally different customer base.

---

### 2. Tech is a Small Part of Magist

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Tech share of orders | ~15% |
| Tech share of revenue | ~14% |
| Tech sellers as % of all sellers | ~15.7% |

Magist is a generalist marketplace. Tech is not its core strength.

---

## Recommendation

Magist is a reliable marketplace — but not the right one for Eniac.

- Tech is only 15% of Magist's business — it is not a tech-focused platform
- Magist buyers are budget-conscious; Eniac's brand is built on premium positioning
- Only ~3% of Magist tech products fall in Eniac's price range (≥€500)
- Entering through Magist risks underpricing, brand dilution, and misaligned customer expectations

**Magist is a good marketplace — just not for Eniac.**

Eniac should explore alternative channels better aligned with its premium identity in Brazil.
